# Getting Started

Welcome to Reply Guys 👋 — the Chrome extension that uses AI to help you reply on **X (Twitter)**, **Farcaster**, and **Binance Square**. It adds a friendly side panel with several automation and monitoring modes, InfoFi score filters, and your choice of AI (OpenAI, Grok, or DeepSeek). This guide walks you through your very first run.

> New here? Install it first with the [Installation Guide](/docs?doc=installation&lang=en) — it takes about 2 minutes.

## 1. Open the Side Panel

First, open a supported site in your browser:

- **X** — [x.com](https://x.com) (or twitter.com)
- **Farcaster** — [farcaster.xyz](https://farcaster.xyz)
- **Binance Square** — [binance.com/square](https://www.binance.com/square)

Then just **click the Reply Guys tab** — the little sparkle logo pinned to the **right edge of the page**. That's the quickest way in.

![The Reply Guys tab sits on the right edge of the page — click it to open the side panel](/docs/images/side-panel-launcher.svg)

The side panel slides open on the right, already set up for the site you're on — the **X panel** on X, the **Farcaster panel** on Farcaster, and the **Binance Square panel** on Binance Square.

> **Two other ways to open it:** click the **Reply Guys icon** in your browser toolbar, or press **Ctrl/Cmd + Shift** with the panel shortcut. The edge tab is usually the easiest.

## 2. Sign In

On first open you'll see the **"Sign in to Reply Guys"** screen. Pick whichever you prefer:

- **Continue with Telegram** — opens `@reply_guys_bot`; press **Start** and you're in.
- **Continue with Discord** — opens Discord authorization; approve it and you're in.

No quota yet? No problem — open **[@reply_guys_bot](https://t.me/reply_guys_bot)**, choose **Buy License**, and pick a plan (there's a free **Trial** to test-drive everything). See [Pricing](#pricing) below.

## 3. Get to Know the Home Hub

Once you're signed in, the panel shows your **home hub**:

- **Your stats (top).** **Quota left** (replies available) and your **Success rate**, with a colored meter for Success / Pending / Failed replies.
- **Monitor** group — **Dashboard**, **Manage KOLs**, **KOL Monitor**.
- **Automate** group — the reply modes (more on these below).
- **Configuration** group — **Settings** and **Account**.

Tap any tile to open it; the back arrow (top-left) always brings you home.

> **On Binance Square,** the very first thing you'll see is a short **"Read before you start"** notice. Please read it and tick the box to continue — automating on Square carries real account risk (see the note under [Binance Square](#binance-square) below).

## 4. Pick a Mode

Reply Guys gives you a few different ways to engage. Pick the one that matches what you're trying to do — you can always switch later.

### Which mode should I use?

| Mode | What it does | Great for | Platform |
|------|--------------|-----------|----------|
| **Post List** | Paste a list of post URLs; it replies to each one | Replying to a specific batch of posts you already have | X (Farcaster: **Reply From Cast List**) |
| **List Reply** | Auto-replies to posts in one of your X **Lists** | Working a curated List timeline | X only |
| **Newfeed** | Replies to posts in your **home feed** or **search results**, with filters | Hands-off engagement across your timeline | X + Farcaster |
| **Reply Repliers** | Paste **your own** post URLs; it replies to the people who **replied to you** | Engaging everyone who commented on your posts | X only |
| **KOL Monitor** | Watches selected KOLs and auto-replies to their **new** posts as they land | Being early to reply to specific accounts | X + Farcaster |

**Rules of thumb:**

- Already have a batch of links? → **Post List** (X) / **Reply From Cast List** (Farcaster)
- Want to work your own timeline or a search query with quality filters? → **Newfeed**
- Want to reply back to people who engaged with you? → **Reply Repliers**
- Want to be early on a handful of key accounts? → **KOL Monitor**

Each mode has its own settings (delay between replies, auto-like, filters, and more). The [Usage Guide](/docs?doc=usage-guide&lang=en) walks through every mode step by step, and [Settings](/docs?doc=settings&lang=en) covers the AI options and the InfoFi score filters.

### Binance Square

Binance Square has its **own panel** with a matching set of modes:

- **Feed** — scan the live Square feed and reply to fresh posts.
- **Link** — paste Square post links (one per line) and reply to each.
- **Search** — reply to posts Square surfaces for a topic.
- **Profile** — reply to a single creator's latest Square posts.
- **KOL monitor** — track selected creators and reply to their new posts (with optional **auto-like**).
- **Daily tasks** — run Square's Rewards-Hub daily tasks for points.

When you're ready, press **Start on Binance Square** (open a Square tab first).

> ⚠️ **Please read:** automating on Binance Square may get your Square **posting/commenting suspended or permanently frozen**, and a Square violation can affect your **KYC-verified account and its funds**. The panel asks you to acknowledge this once before you begin. Use it thoughtfully and at your own discretion.

## 5. Make the Replies Sound Like You (recommended)

Open **Settings → Personalization → Re-analyze Writing Style**. Reply Guys studies your X posts so the AI replies land in your own voice. While you're there, you can also set a **Custom Prompt**, your reply **language**, and **min/max length**.

## Troubleshooting

- **Panel won't open?** → make sure you're on x.com, twitter.com, farcaster.xyz, or binance.com/square, then refresh the page.
- **Can't sign in?** → finish **Start** in `@reply_guys_bot` (or approve Discord); if it times out, just click the button again.
- **"Quota exceeded"?** → top up in `@reply_guys_bot`; you can check your balance under **Account**.
- **A reply didn't post?** → the post may not be repliable, or you were out of quota; the mode's activity log has the details.
